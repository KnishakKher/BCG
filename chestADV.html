CHEST WORKOUT

1 : Push-Ups

Push-ups are performed by placing your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart on the floor, keeping your body straight from head to heels, and lowering your chest until it nearly touches the ground by bending your elbows, then pushing back up to the starting position. They primarily target the chest (pectorals), shoulders (deltoids), and triceps, while also engaging the core and lower back for stability. The benefits include building upper body strength, improving core stability, enhancing muscular endurance, and promoting better posture without the need for any equipment.






2 : Dips

Dips are performed by holding onto parallel bars or the edges of a sturdy surface with your arms straight and body suspended, then slowly lowering yourself by bending your elbows until your upper arms are about parallel to the ground, followed by pushing back up to the starting position. They mainly target the triceps, chest, and front shoulders, while also engaging the core for balance. The benefits include developing strong and defined triceps, improving pushing strength for other exercises like push-ups or bench presses, and enhancing upper body muscle coordination and endurance.
